About

My research focuses on nonlinear dynamics in quantum fluids, particularly Bose–Einstein condensates and spinor superfluids. Using theoretical modelling and numerical simulations of the Gross–Pitaevskii equation, I study interfacial instabilities, vortex dynamics, and pattern formation.

Key topics include Rayleigh–Taylor instability, Kelvin–Helmholtz instability, and counter-superflow instability in multi-component condensates.
